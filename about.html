Hobbies and Activities

skateboarding

Skateboarding is one of my biggest passions. Starting at an early age, I was clueless. I developed new capabilities as years went by. 13 years later I am now a private skateboard instructor located in Jacksonville, Florida. One of the unique things that I love about Skateboarding is that you have to keep practicing in order to be consistent and proper. In my opinion Skateboarding is about being creative and consistent.
